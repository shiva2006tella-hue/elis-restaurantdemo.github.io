Our Story

Where North Africa Meets
the South of France

Eli's is a singular culinary destination in the heart of South Kensington — a place where the rotisserie tradition of southern France meets the vibrant soul of Mediterranean and traditional Algerian cooking. Our corn-fed chickens are imported from France, marinated for 24 hours in a secret blend of herbs and spices, then rotated slowly until golden and succulent.

Our chef prepares everything in house — from handmade couscous to fresh mezze — so that every plate carries the care and craft of a family kitchen. At Eli's, warmth is an ingredient, and every guest is welcomed as family.
